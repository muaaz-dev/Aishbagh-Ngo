About Us

Welcome to AishBagh New Asha Mahila Vikas Samiti, a non-governmental organization dedicated to transforming lives and building stronger communities. Since our establishment in 2007, we have been committed to addressing the most pressing social challenges and providing vital support to the underprivileged members of our society. With a vision of a world where everyone has access to basic necessities and opportunities, we strive to make a tangible difference in the lives of those in need.

Our Mission

At AishBagh New Asha Mahila Vikas Samiti, our mission is to alleviate poverty, promote education, empower women, and support marginalized communities. Through our diverse range of initiatives, we aim to uplift individuals, families, and communities, fostering sustainable growth and enabling them to achieve their full potential.
